Pricing & Billing FAQs

Do you offer a free trial?
Yes. All plans include a 14‑day free trial with no credit card required. You can cancel anytime during the trial.
What happens if I exceed my domain limit?
We’ll send you a notification before you exceed your limit. You can upgrade your plan or remove unused domains.
Can I change plans later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When downgrading, the change will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
Do you offer custom pricing for large enterprises?
Yes. Please contact our sales team and we will prepare a custom plan tailored to your specific requirements.
