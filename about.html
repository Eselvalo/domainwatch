Our Mission

DomainWatch exists to give agencies and MSPs the confidence that their clients' domains are safe, secure and always available.

Our Story

Founded by a team of domain infrastructure experts and former agency owners, DomainWatch was created after countless emergencies caused by expired domains, misconfigured DNS records and broken SSL certificates. We believe that proactive monitoring, clear communication and smart automation can eliminate these incidents and help digital teams focus on what they do best.

Our Values

Trust

We build tools that keep critical assets safe. Trust is at the core of our relationships with customers and partners.

Simplicity

Monitoring should be simple, accessible and actionable. We design intuitive experiences that empower teams.

Transparency

Clear reporting and honest communication help our customers make better decisions and build stronger client relationships.
