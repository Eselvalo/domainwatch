Features that empower your workflow

DomainWatch is packed with intelligent monitoring and collaboration tools to help agencies and MSPs stay ahead of domain issues and deliver exceptional service.

🧭

DNS & Nameservers

Automatically detect A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, TXT and NS record changes and nameserver updates. Stay one step ahead of hijacks and misconfigurations.

  • Record diff & history
  • WHOIS & RDAP monitoring
  • Instant change alerts
🔒

SSL & Security

Monitor certificate validity, chain health and encryption strength. Receive early warnings when certificates are about to expire or misconfigured.

  • Multi-domain & wildcard support
  • TLS handshake & cipher checks
  • OCSP & revocation checks
✉️

Email Deliverability

Validate SPF, DKIM and DMARC records to ensure your clients' emails are authenticated and trustworthy. Get notified when policies are missing or failing.

  • Policy syntax validation
  • MX & SMTP checks
  • DKIM selector inspection
📈

Website & Redirects

Monitor HTTP status codes, detect redirect chains, mixed content and page load anomalies. Ensure your clients' websites are reachable and performant.

  • Reachability & uptime checks
  • Redirect loop detection
  • SSL vs HTTP enforcement
🗂️

Portfolios & Clients

Organize domains by client or brand. Apply tags, assign team members and automate weekly reports for each portfolio.

  • Multi-tenant management
  • Granular user roles
  • Client-facing dashboards
🔔

Notifications & Integrations

Receive alerts via email, Slack, Microsoft Teams or webhooks. Integrate DomainWatch with your incident management and chat tools.

  • Configurable alert policies
  • Daily/weekly digests
  • API & Zapier integrations
