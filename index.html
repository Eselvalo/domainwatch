Frequently asked questions

How does DomainWatch detect changes?
We poll DNS, WHOIS, SSL and HTTP endpoints at frequent intervals using industry‑standard tools and compare the results with your defined baseline. When a change is detected, you'll receive an alert via your preferred channel.
Can I group domains by client?
Yes. You can create portfolios for each client, tag domains and invite collaborators. Each portfolio has its own reports and notification settings.
Do you offer white‑label reporting?
Our Professional and Agency plans include fully customizable, white‑label PDF reports that you can share with your clients. Upload your logo and choose which metrics to include.
